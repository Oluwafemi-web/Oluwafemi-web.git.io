Welcome Address

On behalf of all members of Living Hope Baptist Church, (Testimony Ground), Port Harcourt, I welcome you to the official website of the Church.
We are using this website to introduce you to our church family and the various ministries we engage in. We are a unique set of people from all
works of life brought together by God for signs and wonders.
We are endowed by God with goodly heritage (Psalm 16:6); hence, we are positioned to fulfill the mandate of giving hope to the hopeless in a hopeless world (Job 14:7-9). We are World Changers; people who will transform the world through their God given gifts/talents. We are the salt of the earth Matt. 5:13. We believe that being the salt of the world has a lot to do with giving hope to hopeless around us and that it is not the responsibility of the congregational body called LHBC alone to give hope but also the responsibility of every individual member.
This is very important to us because “the Church is not for entertaining believers, but it is a place to raise up world changers and history makers.” (Sunday Adelaja). For example, Paul and Silas were accused of turning the world upside down (Acts 17:6), we also aspire to change our world for God; therefore, I implore you to join our changing world. Welcome to your moment of IMPARTATION FOR UNCOMMON IMPACTS.

Revd. Dr. Steve O.O. Ayorinde

Church Pastor
